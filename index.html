General Information

Pi Sigma Alpha, the National Political Science Honor Society at CSUF, is a talented group of students dedicated to excelling in the field of political science, in education, and in thier careers. This society recognizes and promotes high academic achievement in political science, providing our members with unique opportunities for scholarly growth, networking, and leadership development.

Our activities range from discussions and guest lectures to community service initiatives and networking events. We assist with the annual Royce Symposium, the University Town Hall, and other campus actvites. Our annual induction banquet is each spring semester.

Requirements

Undergraduate Students:

  • Minimum 3.0 cumulative GPA
  • Minimum 3.25 POSC GPA
  • Completion of ten units in political science
  • At least three upper-division units (300-level or higher)
  • Open to political science majors and minors

Graduate Students:

  • Minimum GPA of 3.5
  • Completion of nine graduate-level units toward the masters degree

$50 Total Membership Fee:

  • $15 Local Chapter Dues:
  • $35 National Dues: Pay directly to Pi Sigma Alpha through their online portal after acceptance and local dues payment.
  • Covers lifetime membership.
  • Local dues must be paid before the application is processed. National registration occurs after acceptance.
